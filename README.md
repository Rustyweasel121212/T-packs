# T-packs
=_+
well what are you looking at this is a texture pack for bloxd.io
if you don't know how to use this texture pack follow the instructions to use this simple t pack that took me less than 1 hr to make
instructions:
1.click code(the green button)
2. click download zip
3. download it to desktop or somewhere you can find
4. open files app
5. open the zip
6. drag out the 2 folders texture and skyboxes and anything else
7.create a new folder name it and drag the files in
8. open bloxd.io
9. press o
10. goto texture pack and put the pack in
11. you got the pack
